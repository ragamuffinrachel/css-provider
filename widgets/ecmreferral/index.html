ECM Referral Process

Referral processes are available for Providers, members, caregivers, and family.

Complete ECM Screening Assessment PP via KHS Provider Portal.

  • Community Based Organizations (Non‑contracted providers)
    • Submit an ECM referral form:
      • By email to ECMOutreachSpecialist@khs-net.com
      • By fax to 661‑473‑7501
  • Members/Caregivers/Family
    • Can self-refer by calling KFHC at 1‑800‑391‑2000 or 661‑632‑1590 and selecting option 4 to speak with an ECM Outreach Specialist.
  • For all Referrals: ECM Nursing Staff will validate ECM eligibility based on the completed assessment and proceed with the standard authorization or denial procedure.
